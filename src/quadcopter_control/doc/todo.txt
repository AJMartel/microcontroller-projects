sticks
LY		pitch -90º to +90º (limited by mode)
LX		roll -90º to +90º (limited by mode)
RY		throttle 0 to 100%
RX		
L3		reset attitude (only when disarmed)
R3		calibrate gyros and accels (only when disarmed)

mode buttons
start		arm / disarm (safety limits applied to arming, disarm is a kill switch)
select		
analog		n/a

top buttons
L1		-5º / sec
L2		-15º / sec
L1+L2		-45º / sec
R1		+5º / sec
R2		+15º / sec
R1+R2		+45º / sec

right buttons
square		aerobatic / 3d mode (no limits on pitch and roll)
triangle	sport mode (roll limited to 30º, pitch forward limited to 45º, no pitch backwards)
circle		
ex		normal mode (pitch and roll limited to 5º)
